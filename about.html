About YIAscm.CampusShield

**Our Mission:** To ensure a welcoming, safe, and respectful environment for every student. CampusShield provides a secure and confidential platform for immediate reporting and transparent action against all forms of ragging and harassment.

This initiative is part of the University's **Zero-Tolerance Policy** against ragging, mandated by the UGC (Prevention and Prohibition of Ragging in Higher Educational Institutions) Regulations. Every member of the academic community, from faculty to staff and students, is responsible for preventing any form of abuse.

Anti-Ragging Committee

The Anti-Ragging Committee is the supreme body responsible for ensuring the compliance of anti-ragging measures and taking stringent disciplinary action when necessary.
